Patents Overview

Patents Overview

KATEM continuously registers its technology development Product and technologies for Research & Development both domestically and internationally to protect them and prevent infringement.
Particularly, the Research & Development on surface crack prevention manufacturing technology for High-speed Rail forged steel brake discs is the world's first and represents a leading technology abroad. It has been registered not only in Korea but also in High-speed Rail producing countries including China and EU member states such as France, Germany, Italy, and Spain.
Currently, KATEM holds a total of 12 registered Research & Development items: 10 domestic and 2 overseas. In addition, it holds 2 design R&D registrations and 2 trademark registrations, totaling 16 intellectual property rights.
Moreover, KATEM has 1 domestic application and 2 PCT applications in progress.
