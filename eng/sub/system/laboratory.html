Laboratory Overview

Research Institute Overview

KATEM has established and operates a corporate research institute to continuously advance technological innovation and strengthen Research & Development capabilities.
Based on rigorous quality control and cutting-edge design/manufacturing technologies, we aim to develop globally competitive Product and build a smart manufacturing environment.
