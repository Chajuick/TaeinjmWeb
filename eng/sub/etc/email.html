Privacy Statement

All personal information handled by KATEM is collected, retained, and processed in accordance with applicable laws or with the consent of the data subject.
In compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act, such personal information will be lawfully and appropriately handled to ensure the proper execution of public duties and to protect the rights of individuals.
KATEM respects your rights under applicable laws, including the right to request access to personal information and the right to request correction or deletion. You may file an administrative appeal regarding any infringement of these rights in accordance with the Administrative Appeals Act.
KATEM’s privacy practices consist of the “Website User Personal Information Handling Policy” to protect users’ personal information on KATEM’s website, and the “Computer-Processed Personal Information Handling Policy” required for performing official tasks.
We appreciate your use of the KATEM website and provide this explanation regarding the website’s privacy practices.
This policy is based on the current Personal Information Protection Act, the Act on the Protection of Personal Information in Public Institutions, and KATEM’s Privacy Policy.
Unless otherwise stated, this policy applies only to KATEM’s official website, which is katem.co.kr.

Automatically Collected and Stored Information

When you use the KATEM website, the following information is automatically collected and stored:

  • Your internet domain and the addresses of websites visited prior to accessing our site
  • Your browser type and operating system (OS)
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is used to improve the website, analyze statistics, and facilitate communication between users and the site. However, certain disclosures may be required by law.

Information Collected via Email and Web Forms

Users may provide personal information through mail, phone, or online forms. Please note the following:

  • Information entered on the website may be viewed by others.
  • Provided information may be shared with others or used for policy development and data analysis in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Information may be shared with other government departments or provided as necessary.
  • While administrative and technical measures are implemented to secure the website, avoid entering sensitive information.
  • Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their personal ID and password, and should change them regularly to prevent unauthorized access.

Website Security Measures

KATEM implements the following measures to ensure website security and continuous service:

  • Network traffic control and monitoring
  • Programs to detect unauthorized information modification attempts

Linked Sites and Web Pages

If you click on links or banners leading to other sites, the privacy policies of those sites will apply. Please review the respective site’s policy.

Prohibition of Obtaining Others’ Personal Information

Acquiring personal information such as email addresses of others through KATEM’s website is prohibited. Unauthorized access or provision of personal information may result in penalties under applicable laws.
